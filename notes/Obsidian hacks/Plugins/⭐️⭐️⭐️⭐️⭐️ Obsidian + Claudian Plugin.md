# Claudian

**GitHub:** https://github.com/claudiusio/obsidian-claudian

## What is Claudian?

**Claudian** brings the power of **Claude AI** directly into Obsidian, transforming your note-taking into an intelligent, collaborative experience. It lets you:

- 🤖 Get AI assistance without leaving your vault
- ✍️ Write, edit, and improve your notes with AI suggestions
- 💡 Brainstorm ideas and expand your thoughts instantly
- 🔍 Summarize and analyze your notes intelligently
- 📚 Connect ideas across your vault with AI insights
- 🎯 Generate outlines, lists, and structured content
- 🚀 Accelerate your writing and thinking process

## How Claudian Works

1. **Select text** — Highlight content in your note you want AI assistance with
2. **Choose an action** — Pick from built-in commands or create custom ones
3. **Get instant help** — Claude processes your request and returns results
4. **Edit and refine** — Accept, modify, or iterate on the AI suggestions
5. **Continue working** — All within your Obsidian vault, uninterrupted

## Example: The Full Workflow

**Step 1: Capture an idea in your note**
```markdown
# New Product Feature Ideas

## E-commerce Enhancement
I think we should improve the checkout process somehow. 
Users seem frustrated. Maybe faster or simpler?
```

**Step 2: Select the section and run "Brainstorm"**

**Step 3: Claude expands your thought**
```markdown
## E-commerce Enhancement
I think we should improve the checkout process somehow. 
Users seem frustrated. Maybe faster or simpler?

**AI Suggestions:**
- One-click checkout with saved payment methods
- Guest checkout option (no account required)
- Real-time cart cost preview
- Estimated shipping calculation upfront
- Multiple payment method support
- Mobile-optimized checkout flow
- Progress indicator for multi-step checkout
```

**Step 4: Keep what resonates, refine further**
```markdown
## E-commerce Enhancement
**Priority improvements:**
1. One-click checkout (returning customers)
2. Guest checkout (lower friction)
3. Mobile optimization (60% of traffic)
```
