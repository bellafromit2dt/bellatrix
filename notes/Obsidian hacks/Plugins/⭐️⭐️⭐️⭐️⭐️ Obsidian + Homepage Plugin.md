# Homepage

**GitHub:** https://github.com/mirnovov/obsidian-homepage

## What is the Homepage Plugin?

**Homepage** lets you create a **beautiful, personalized dashboard** that opens when you launch Obsidian. Instead of staring at a blank page, you get:

- 🏠 A custom landing page with your favorite links and quick access
- 📊 Widgets displaying dynamic content (recent notes, tasks, calendar, etc.)
- 🎯 Quick navigation to your most-used sections
- 🎨 Beautiful customizable layouts and themes
- ⚡ Fast access to everything you care about without scrolling
- 📌 Pinned notes, folders, and searches at your fingertips

## Why Use the Homepage Plugin?

**Homepage transforms Obsidian from a note-taking app into a personal operating system:**

- **Faster startup** — Jump directly to what you need instead of hunting through folders
- **Better organization** — Create a visual map of your vault at a glance
- **Motivation boost** — See your progress, goals, and recent work immediately
- **Customizable dashboard** — Design it exactly how you want
- **Improved workflow** — Reduce friction and unnecessary clicks
- **Personal touch** — Make your Obsidian feel like YOUR space

## Key Features at a Glance

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|------------|
| **Custom Landing Page** | Beautiful homepage opens when you start Obsidian |
| **Quick Links** | Pin your most-used notes, searches, and folders |
| **Widgets** | Display dynamic content (recent files, calendar, etc.) |
| **Layouts** | Choose between different design templates |
| **Customizable Styling** | Adjust colors, fonts, and spacing to your taste |
| **Hotkey Support** | Jump to homepage with a keyboard shortcut |
| **Multiple Tabs** | Create different homepage layouts for different workflows |
| **Markdown Support** | Write custom content using Markdown |

## How It Works

1. **Install & enable** — Get the plugin running in your vault
2. **Open settings** — Configure your homepage layout
3. **Add content** — Add quick links, widgets, and personalized sections
4. **Customize** — Adjust styling and appearance to match your taste
5. **Enjoy** — Every time you open Obsidian, your dashboard greets you!
