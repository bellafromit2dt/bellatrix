# Habit Tracker 21

**GitHub:** https://github.com/zoreet/obsidian-habit-tracker

## What is Habit Tracker 21?

**Habit Tracker 21** helps you build and maintain habits with visual progress tracking right in Obsidian. It lets you:

- 📊 Track daily habits with a visual calendar grid
- 🎯 See your streaks and consistency at a glance
- 📈 Visualize progress over weeks and months
- 🏆 Monitor habit completion rates and patterns
- 💪 Stay motivated with streak tracking
- 📝 Link habits to your notes and goals
- 🔄 Create recurring habits and reminders
- 📅 Review habit history and evolution

## Why Use Habit Tracker 21?

**Habit Tracker 21 makes building habits visible and rewarding:**

- **Visual motivation** — See your streaks and celebrate progress
- **Accountability** — Daily tracking keeps you honest
- **Pattern recognition** — Identify what works and what doesn't
- **Context awareness** — Habits live in your vault, linked to your goals
- **No app switching** — Track habits without leaving Obsidian
- **Persistent data** — All habit data is backed up with your vault
- **Reflection space** — Link habit notes to reflections and learning
- **Science-backed** — The 21-day concept plus streaks drive behavior change

## How Habit Tracker 21 Works

1. **Create a habit** — Define what you want to track (exercise, meditation, reading, etc.)
2. **Set frequency** — Daily, specific days, or custom schedule
3. **Log daily** — Check off completion each day
4. **Watch progress** — See streaks and visual calendar fill up
5. **Review patterns** — Analyze what's working and adjust

## Example: Setting Up a Habit

**In your Obsidian note:**
```markdown
# Daily Habits

## Morning Routine

### Meditation
- Habit: Daily meditation (10 minutes)
- Target: Every day
- Started: 2026-07-01
- Current Streak: 9 days

[Habit Tracker showing calendar grid with check marks]

## Exercise

### Gym Session
- Habit: Workout
- Target: 3x per week
- Current Streak: 2 days
- Completion Rate: 67%

[Habit Tracker showing calendar grid]

## Learning

### Reading
- Habit: Read for 30 minutes
- Target: Daily
- Current Streak: 5 days
- Linked: [[reading-goals]]

[Habit Tracker showing calendar grid]
```

## Tips for Habit Success

💡 **Start small** — One habit per week for sustainable growth
💡 **Stack habits** — Attach new habits to existing ones
💡 **Track immediately** — Mark completion same day for accuracy
💡 **Review weekly** — Sunday review of the week's progress
💡 **Link to motivation** — Connect habits to your bigger goals
💡 **Be consistent** — Missing one day is a missed habit; missing two is a new habit
💡 **Celebrate streaks** — Milestone celebrations (7 days, 30 days, 100 days!)

## Habit Stacking Strategy

**Powerful technique: Chain habits together**

```markdown
## Morning Habit Stack (15 minutes)
After: Wake up → Immediately start

1. Meditation (5 min) → triggers
2. Journaling (5 min) → triggers  
3. Reading (5 min) → start day

All tracked in one morning routine check
Better than three separate habit checks
```

## Tracking & Analysis

**Monitor your progress:**
- Daily completion rate
- Current streaks (for each habit)
- Longest streak achieved
- Patterns (best days, most consistent habits)
- Calendar heatmap (when you're most consistent)

**Use with Dataview:**
```dataview
TABLE habit, "Current Streak", "Completion %"
FROM #habit
WHERE status = "active"
SORT "Current Streak" DESC
```
